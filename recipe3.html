Vegetable Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 cup snap peas, ends trimmed
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil for cooking
  • Cooked rice or noodles for serving
  • Sesame seeds and chopped green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Set aside.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Add broccoli, bell pepper, carrot, zucchini, and snap peas to the wok. Stir-fry for 4-5 minutes or until vegetables are tender-crisp.
  4. Pour the prepared sauce over the vegetables and toss to coat evenly. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  5. Serve the vegetable stir fry over cooked rice or noodles.
  6. Garnish with sesame seeds and chopped green onions before serving.
