Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 1 celery stalk, finely chopped
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup whole milk or heavy cream
  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Drain excess fat if necessary.
  2. Add the chopped onion, minced garlic, grated carrot, and chopped celery. Cook until vegetables are softened.
  3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, red wine (if using), oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 30-45 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Meanwhile, cook the spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  5. Stir the milk or heavy cream into the Bolognese sauce and cook for an additional 10 minutes.
  6. Serve the Bolognese sauce over the cooked spaghetti. Sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese before serving.
